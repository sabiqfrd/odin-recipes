Roast Chicken

Description

These air fryer roast chicken is thankfully easy to follow with ingredients you can normally find in a kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg whole chicken at room temperature, giblets and neck removed
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 3 fresh whole rosemary sprigs
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towel.
  3. Brush completely with butter.
  4. Season with sea salt and cracked pepper and lots of lovely fresh rosemary.
  5. Place chicken into air fryer basket.
  6. Cook at 180°C, for 50-60 minutes.
  7. Let the roast chicken rest for a few minutes before removing carefully with tongs.
